Our Services

Embroidery

If embroidery is required, please send us your logo and we can cost individually to your requirements, embroidered logos are long lasting and extremely neat but can be slightly more expensive than screenprinting. Logos can be placed anywhere on the garments and there are no colour minimums necessary. All logos will be digitized and sampled and sent to our customers for approval before applying them onto the garments.

Manufacturing

Santon Workwear manufactures all overalls and workwear in our Hammarsdale factory in KZN. We do not import any made up overall. That being so, as a customer you are dealing with a factory and as a factory we require minimums in order to manufacture non stock items. Minimums of 100 units per colour per style are required to run special order through the factory. All stock items can be placed as and when you require and we will not force you to buy boxed lots.

Screenprinting

We operate our own in-house screenprinting room and can print up to 6 colour logos. All logos are digitized and approved with our clients before applying them onto the garments.
